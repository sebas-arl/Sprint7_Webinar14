## Key Projects

### 1. Interactive Biometric Classification Dashboard (Penguins)
**Focus:** Statistical Analysis & Data Visualization.
* **The Problem:** Understanding how physical measurements (bill length, flippers, body mass) determine penguin species and their respective habitats.
* **The Solution:** Developed a Streamlit dashboard that enables real-time data filtering to identify growth and feeding patterns by species.
* **Tech Stack:** Data Wrangling (Pandas), Interactive Viz (Seaborn/Matplotlib), and Deployment (Render).

### 2. Probability & Prediction Simulator (Coin Flip)
**Focus:** Simulation Logic & Descriptive Statistics.
* **The Problem:** Demonstrating the Law of Large Numbers and the stability of frequencies in random experiments.
* **The Solution:** Built a Monte Carlo simulation tool that executes thousands of trials to analyze probability convergence.
* **Tech Stack:** Computational logic, NumPy for simulations, and statistical metrics.

### 3. Consumer Behavior Analytics (Restaurant Tips)
**Focus:** Business Strategy & Revenue Optimization.
* **The Problem:** Identifying which factors (service quality, product type, time of day) maximize customer experience and, consequently, tip percentages.
* **The Solution:** Conducted a correlation analysis to propose "Customer Experience" strategies aimed at increasing average ticket size and loyalty.
* **Tech Stack:** Correlation analysis, data segmentation, and strategic marketing thinking.
